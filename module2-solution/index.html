---Our Menu---


Chicken

We are proud to be one of the first fully Halal international quick service restaurant (QSR) brands. We serve fresh, great tasting chicken, using only the finest quality herbs and spices, carefully selected from around the world, to deliver exciting flavors in a modern setting. What makes our chicken the best is that we only use the best parts, the chicken breast. Following halal practices, the meat is always manually deboned by our approved suppliers.

Beef

The beef in our menu is always 100% pure and halal. The only thing we add is a dash of salt and pepper. So no additives, preservatives or anything else. The vegetables and salads are as fresh as they come. All the produce is rinsed and cleaned by our approved suppliers before being sent to the restaurants and served on your tray.Just beef as it should be..

Sushi
